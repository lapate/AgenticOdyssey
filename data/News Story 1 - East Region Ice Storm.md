# BREAKING: Historic Ice Storm Paralyzes Eastern Seaboard — Zava Supply Chain Faces Major Disruption

**ZavaTimes Business Desk** | *February 25, 2026*

---

## Unprecedented Winter Storm "Glacier" Slams East Coast for Second Week

A once-in-a-generation ice storm, dubbed "Glacier" by the National Weather Service, has brought the Eastern United States to a near standstill, with widespread road closures, power outages affecting over 4 million households, and a complete shutdown of freight rail operations from Virginia to Maine.

For **Zava**, the athletic performance brand, the timing couldn't be worse. The East region represents over **$2.5 million in annual revenue** and is home to some of the company's most critical B2B partners, including **Fabrikam, Inc.** — Zava's single largest customer at **$1.08 million in total revenue** — as well as **VanArsdel, Ltd.**, **Bellows College**, and **Adatum Corporation**.

## Delivery Delays Mount

Zava's fulfillment operations have confirmed that **all East region deliveries are suspended indefinitely**. This impacts roughly **760 active orders** across B2B and B2C channels. The company's primary East Coast distribution hub in Newark, NJ has been operating on backup generators since Tuesday, and UPS and FedEx have both issued force majeure notices for the affected corridor.

> *"We're looking at a minimum two-week backlog once conditions improve,"* said a Zava logistics spokesperson. *"Our priority is ensuring product integrity — especially for temperature-sensitive ZavaCore Fiber raw materials sitting in unheated trailers."*

## Key Customers Sound the Alarm

**Fabrikam, Inc.**, which regularly orders large volumes of **ZavaCore Fiber — Field Grade** raw materials and **Systems Custom Team Jerseys**, has warned that production at its East Coast manufacturing facilities could halt by next week if materials don't arrive. Fabrikam alone accounts for **37 orders** with Zava and depends on consistent raw material deliveries to fulfill its own downstream contracts.

**Bellows College**, another major East region account with **39 orders** primarily for custom team uniforms, has reportedly postponed its spring athletics season launch, citing uniform delivery uncertainty.

**Adatum Corporation** and **VanArsdel, Ltd.** — representing a combined **$881,000+ in revenue** — have both reached out to Zava requesting expedited alternatives, including air freight from West region warehouses, though capacity remains extremely limited.

## Financial Impact Estimates

Analysts estimate the storm could cost Zava between **$300,000 and $500,000** in delayed revenue recognition this quarter alone, with potential additional losses from:

- **Spoilage risk** for ZavaCore Fiber shipments ($25,500+ per typical Fabrikam order)
- **Contractual penalties** for late B2B deliveries
- **Lost B2C impulse purchases** via Direct eCommerce as consumers shift spending to storm essentials

## Zava's Response

Zava has activated its emergency logistics protocol, rerouting what inventory it can through Central region distribution channels via **Wide World Importers'** warehouse network. The company is also offering affected B2C customers a 15% discount on future orders and waiving all shipping fees for East region deliveries through March.

*The company's stock dipped 3.2% in early trading but recovered slightly by midday as investors weighed the temporary nature of the disruption against Zava's diversified regional footprint.*
